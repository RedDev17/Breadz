Cakes to bake this Christmas




You may find a ton of amazing Christmas cake ideas here that will impress your guests, family, and friends. Every single one of them is bursting with festive flavours such as gingerbread, cranberry, peppermint, and even a cake that tastes exactly like pecan pie. In addition, there are dessert suggestions with coconut, caramel, and chocolate. For you traditionalists out there, we've also included a fruit cake. There are countless flavour combinations available for Christmas cakes.

1 - Chocolate Bundt Cake



Any occasion is appropriate for a traditional chocolate Bundt cake. Add some Christmas sprinkles on top of the ganache layer, and you have a very festive holiday version!

2 - Chocolate Raspberry Cake



Even without decorations, a tall tier cake is stunning. You can elevate this dish to a whole new level of impressiveness by adding chocolate curls on top for an extra-indulgent touch!

3 - Sour Cream Coffee Cake



Christmas morning is the perfect excuse if you've ever needed one to eat cake for breakfast! We won't blame you for going back for a second sliceâ€”the luxurious cake and the crumbly crunch of the streusel are just too good.

4 - Eclair Cake



This chocolate tenderness has the exact flavour of a freshly baked good from France! Graham crackers, a smooth filling, and a rich chocolate ganache are stacked inside.

5 - Gingerbread Cake



This cake is perfect for you if you enjoy gingerbread biscuits! It features an incredible fluffy lemon cream cheese icing along with all of the same toasty spices!

6 - Chocolate Swiss Roll Cake



Cakes with Swiss rolls are always delicious! You will experience childhood memories with the swirls of cream filling and chocolate cake.

7 - Coffee-Toffee Crunch Cake



Coffee as dessert is a great way to cap off your holiday meal! This three-layer cake is really special because of the crispy toffee bars on top and the delicious icing.

8 - Black Forest Cake



Cherries and chocolate go together like peanuts and honey! The layers of rich chocolate cake in this stunning layer cake are piled with handmade whipped cream and tangy cherry filling.

9 - Red Velvet Cake



Though it's tasty all year long, red velvet seems particularly suitable at Christmas. Remember to add sugared cranberries and rosemary sprigs for decoration.

10 - Cinnamon Roll Cake



What a clever way to put a holiday staple treat. Perfect as a special treat on Christmas morning, this cake makes a wonderful holiday dessert.
